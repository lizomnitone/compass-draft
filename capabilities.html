Roll Bending

▼

Roll bending uses rotating rollers to create smooth, large-radius curves. Our manual and CNC machines handle diverse profiles and complex geometries, from simple arcs to compound and multi-plane bends.

Capabilities Specifications
Profiles Solid bars (round, square, flat), tubes (round (pipe), square), brackets, channels, beams, plate, custom extruded profiles.
Materials Carbon steel, stainless steel, aluminum, copper, brass, nickel alloys, other workable metals.
Profile Sizes ~1/4" to 5" pipe; 6" and 8" pipe coming soon.
Bend Radii Material and profile dependent; ~2D minimum to essentially flat maximum. Please inquire for specific applications.
Complex Bends Yes; compound bends and multi-plane bends.
Common Applications Structural components, pipeline bends, architectural/artistic work, handrails, platforms, ornamental designs.

Equipment Used

Equipment Type Description & Application
Manual Roll Bender Traditional roll bending machine with manual adjustment. Operator controls roller position and material feed. Ideal for smaller projects and custom work.
CNC Roll Bender Computer-controlled system with automatic roller adjustment and material feed. Provides precise, repeatable bends with excellent consistency.
Roller Dies Specialized roller dies for creating complex profiles and unique geometries. Custom dies can be made for specific applications.

Draw Bending

▼

Draw bending creates precise, tight-radius bends using mandrels and specialized dies. Ideal for complex geometries, heavy or thin pipe and tube, and intricate compound bends. We have hundreds of dies on hand for common sizes, or if you need something custom, we make tooling in our in-house machine shop.

Capabilities Specifications
Profiles Pipe, hollow round, square, and rectangular tube, custom hollow profiles
Materials Carbon steel, stainless steel, aluminum, copper, brass, nickel alloys, other workable materials
Pipe Diameter Range 2" to 16" diameter pipe (larger sizes up to 66" available by subcontract)
Bend Radii 15D, 20D, 50D, and elbows (3D, 5D, etc.) - where D = nominal diameter. For example: 4" O.D. @ 20D = 80" centerline radius
Complex Bends Yes; compound bends and multi-plane bends
Common Applications Precision applications with minimal distortion; Structural components, pipeline bends, artistic and ornamental pieces, high-precision assemblies

Equipment Used

Equipment Type Description & Application
Manual Draw Bending Machine Dedicated machine with clamp to grip material, mandrel support system, and die holder. Designed specifically for draw bending of pipe and tube. Operator controls bending parameters for custom applications.
CNC Draw Bending Machine Computer-controlled draw bending machine for complex, repeatable bends with high precision and consistency. Automatic control of clamp pressure, bend angle, and material feed.
Mandrels Internal support tools in multiple types: bullet nose, plug, and ball mandrel. Stocked to support a variety of profile shapes and sizes. Prevents wall collapse during bending.
Dies Shaped tools that form the outer profile of the bend. Hundreds of standard dies kept in inventory for common radii and profiles. Custom dies made in-house as required.

Compression Bending

▼

Compression bending uses a bending table and specialized hydraulic press systems to produce consistent, repeatable bends in pipeline, structural aluminum, and other heavy-wall sections. Primary applications include pipeline work and architectural/structural aluminum components.

Capabilities Specifications
Profiles Solid bars (round, square, flat), tubes (round (pipe), square), brackets, channels, beams, plate, custom extruded profiles.
Materials Carbon steel, aluminium, copper, brass, other workable material
Pipe/Section Sizes Minimum ~1/4" to 16" pipe and larger sections.
Bend Radii Minimum ~2T (solid sections, where T = thickness; see glossary) or ~2D (hollow & other sections, where D = diameter). Maximum is essentially flat/straight.
Common Applications Pipelines, structural shapes, brackets, clamps, and architectural structural aluminum components.

Equipment Used

Equipment Type Description & Application
Bending Table(s) Large steel table with hydraulic press for producing multiple identical bends consistently. Supports both manual and CNC-controlled setups. Primary equipment for pipeline and large-section bending.
Hydraulic Cylinders Precision-controlled hydraulic systems that drive the bending process with accuracy and consistency.
Compression Dies Standard dies for common pipe sizes and radii. Stock dies available for expedited production.
Specialized Compression Bending Machines Custom-built equipment for specific applications, including electrical conduit benders and aluminum bumper bending machinery.
Custom Support Machinery Integrated and specialized support equipment designed for unique compression bending applications.
Integrated Compression Bender Specialized systems combining bending, hydraulic control, and support machinery into a single integrated unit for streamlined production.

Coil Bending

▼

Custom coil bending for continuous curves and complex geometries. Ideal for tight-pitch coils and specialized applications. Typically used in industrial and chemical processing, as well as custom projects requiring precision coiling.

Capabilities Specifications
Profiles Round (hollow or solid), square tube, angle iron, solid bars, flat bars, channels
Materials Stainless steel, mild steel, copper, brass, other workable metals
Bend Radii Material and profile dependent; ~1/4" to 40 feet (generally limited by material length rather than machine capability)
Coil Configurations Tight pitch coils, near-spirals, continuous curves, complex geometries, custom specifications
Common Applications Industrial and chemical processing equipment, specialized mechanical components, gizmos and gadgets

Equipment Used

Equipment Type Description & Application
Coil Bending Machine Specialized equipment with rotating mandrel or form. Material is fed and wrapped continuously to create spiral patterns. Pitch and diameter precisely controlled.
Custom Forms & Mandrels Specialized tools designed for specific coil applications. Can be made to exact specifications for unique project requirements.
Variable Speed Drive Allows precise control of winding speed and material feed rate, enabling consistent coil spacing and geometry.

Compass Bending operates under and is familiar with recognized industry standards that ensure quality, safety, and compliance for every project.

Applicable Standards & Codes

Compass Bending conforms to all applicable codes and standards. We specialize in CSA and ASME/ASTM standards for pressure pipe and structural applications, with extensive inventory and testing capability for sour-service pipeline work.

CSA Inspection & Testing Standards

Standard Scope Description
CSA Z245.11 Steel pipe — seamless and welded Canadian standard covering material specifications, manufacturing, testing, and inspection requirements for steel pipe used in pressure and non-pressure applications
CSA Z245.16 Steel pipe — seamless and welded (sour service) Hydrogen sulfide (H₂S) resistant requirements for pipeline and pressure service applications in corrosive environments

Other Standards
