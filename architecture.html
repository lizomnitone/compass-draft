Architecture

Precision metal bending for architectural design and construction.

Overview

Compass Bending delivers precision metal components for architectural applications, from structural support elements to decorative features. Our expertise in bending diverse profiles enables architects and builders to realize their design visions with quality and reliability.

Applications

Architectural projects depend on precision bending for both functional and aesthetic metal components.

Application Typical Uses Bending Solutions
Structural Elements Curved beams, arches, support frames, load-bearing components Large-radius bending, structural steel, precision to specification, integration with fabrication.
Railings & Handrails Staircase railings, balcony guards, safety railings, artistic railings Elegant curves, stainless steel and carbon steel, welding integration, finishing coordination.
Facade & Cladding Curved metal panels, screen elements, decorative features Complex curved profiles, custom radii, multiple identical pieces for consistency.
Interior Design Curved partitions, custom fixtures, architectural accents Design collaboration, diverse materials, finishing options to match aesthetic vision.

Design & Technical Support

We work with architects and design teams to translate designs into buildable components. Our bending expertise can provide valuable guidance on feasibility, material selection, and production methods for metal components.

Consulting Services

Service Description
Design Consultation Feedback on design feasibility and optimization from a bending and fabrication perspective
Material Guidance Recommendations on material selection for strength, durability, and aesthetics
Technical Specifications Expert guidance on achievable tolerances, bend radii, and finish requirements
Production Planning Coordination with fabrication, welding, and finishing for seamless delivery

Quality & Specifications

Architectural components must meet project specifications and applicable standards. We ensure all work meets design requirements and documentation standards.

Testing & Inspection

Test/Inspection Type Purpose

Material Certifications Mill test reports and documentation for code compliance
