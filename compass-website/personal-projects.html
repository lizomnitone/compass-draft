Personal Projects

Custom metal bending for hobbies, DIY projects, and personal creations.

Overview

We love working on personal projects. Whether you're building a custom frame for your hot rod, creating a unique piece of furniture, or working on a weekend hobby project, we treat every project—no matter the size—as an important opportunity for collaboration and creativity.

Why Choose Compass for Your Project

Personal projects are special. They reflect your vision and creativity, and we're committed to making them a reality.

What Makes Us Different

Benefit What This Means for You
We Do Small Jobs Every project matters to us, regardless of size or complexity. We're happy to work with individuals on one-off pieces.
Flexible Approach We work with your sketches, napkin drawings, or detailed CAD files. Bring us what you've got and we'll help make it real.
Material Flexibility We can source material for you, or work with material you supply. We'll quote labor and material separately if you prefer.
Creative Collaboration Stuck on a technical problem? We can offer suggestions based on our experience and help optimize your design.
Real People Answer When you call, you'll talk to a real person who understands your project and can discuss options with you.

How We Work With You

We keep the process simple and collaborative, working at your pace and budget.

The Process

Step What Happens
1. Initial Chat Call or email us with your idea. We'll ask questions to understand your vision and scope.
2. Design & Planning We'll discuss technical approach, material options, and timeline. Bring sketches or drawings if you have them.
3. Quote We'll provide a quote for labor and material (quoted separately if you prefer). No surprises.
4. Production Once you approve, we get to work. We'll keep you updated on progress.
5. Finish & Delivery Pick up from our shop or we can arrange delivery. If you want finishing work (painting, grinding, etc.), we can coordinate that too.
