Build Your Career With Us

At Compass Bending, we're passionate about precision metal bending and the people who make it happen. We're always looking for driven, curious, and collaborative individuals to join our growing team in Calgary, Alberta and across North America.

Why Join Compass Bending?

Meaningful Work

Work on projects that matter—from infrastructure to innovation. Your skills make a real impact on our customers' success.

Team & Culture

Join a supportive, collaborative team of over 30 experienced professionals who value integrity, continuous learning, and hands-on problem solving.

Growth Opportunities

We invest in our team. You'll have opportunities to develop new skills, take on greater responsibility, and advance your career.

Competitive Benefits

Enjoy competitive compensation, flexible work arrangements, and a workplace that values your contributions.

Our Hiring Process

We believe in finding the right fit for both the role and our team. Here's what to expect:

1. Application

Submit your resume and tell us why you're interested in joining Compass Bending. Use our contact form or email directly.

2. Initial Review

Our team reviews your qualifications and experience to ensure alignment with the role and our values.

3. Interview

Selected candidates will be invited for a conversation (virtual or in-person) to learn more about you and your goals.

4. Offer

If it's a great fit, we'll extend an offer. We're excited to have you join the team!
