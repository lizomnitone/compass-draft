Industrial Manufacturing

Precision bending solutions for manufacturing and equipment production.

Overview

Compass Bending provides specialized metal bending solutions for industrial manufacturing applications. From equipment frames to complex assemblies, our precision work ensures reliability and quality in your production processes.

Applications

Industrial manufacturing relies on precision bending for a wide range of critical components and assemblies.

Application Typical Uses Bending Solutions
Equipment Frames Support structures, chassis, mounting systems Precision bends in carbon steel, stainless steel. Custom radii and angles. Structural reinforcement bends. Quick turnaround for production timelines.
Conveyor Systems Frame assemblies, support arms, guide rails Consistent radius bending, multiple identical pieces, integration with fabrication services.
Machine Components Brackets, guards, shrouds, protective covers Complex geometries, tight tolerances, coordination with machining and assembly.

Quality & Standards
