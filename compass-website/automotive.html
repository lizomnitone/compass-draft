Automotive

Precision metal bending for automotive components and systems.

Overview

Compass Bending serves the automotive industry with precision metal bending solutions for exhaust systems, suspension components, structural supports, and specialized assemblies. Our expertise ensures quality and reliability for both OEM and aftermarket applications.

Applications

Automotive applications demand precision, consistency, and reliability for critical components.

Application Typical Uses Bending Solutions
Exhaust Systems Muffler bends, intake tubes, custom piping Stainless steel bending, tight radius bends, heat-resistant materials, high-volume production capability.
Suspension Components Control arms, stabilizer bars, suspension tubing Precision bends to exact specifications, multiple identical pieces, integration with fabrication services.
Structural Supports Frame reinforcements, protective guards, mounting brackets Complex geometries, tight tolerances, coordination with welding and assembly.

Quality & Standards

Automotive components require stringent quality control and documentation to meet industry standards and OEM specifications.

Testing & Inspection

Test/Inspection Type Purpose
Dimensional Inspection Verification of bend radii, angles, wall thickness, and overall dimensions
Material Certifications Complete traceability documentation for OEM requirements
Pressure & Leak Testing Testing for fuel and fluid system components to specification
Visual Inspection Surface quality and finish verification

Industry Standards

Standard/Code Description
ASTM Material specifications and mechanical testing standards
SAE Society of Automotive Engineers standards
OEM Specifications Customer-specific engineering and manufacturing requirements

Shipping & Logistics

We manage shipments for automotive components with attention to delivery timelines and protection requirements. Our fleet and carrier partnerships ensure reliable delivery to manufacturing facilities and assembly plants.

Shipping Options

Service Type Description
Full Truck Load (FTL) Dedicated truck shipments for production runs
Less Than Truck Load (LTL) Flexible options for smaller shipments
Just-In-Time Delivery Coordinated delivery to match assembly line schedules
