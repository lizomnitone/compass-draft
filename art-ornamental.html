Art & Ornamental

Custom metal bending for artistic and decorative applications.

Overview

Compass Bending brings artistic visions to life with custom metal bending services for sculptures, installations, and ornamental work. Our combination of technical expertise and creative collaboration makes us ideal partners for artists and designers looking to realize ambitious projects in metal.

Applications

From concept to completion, we support creative metal bending projects of all scales and complexities.

Application Typical Uses Bending Solutions
Sculptures Abstract forms, figurative work, large-scale installations Custom curves and complex geometries, wide range of materials, artistic finishes, one-of-a-kind pieces.
Decorative Railings Staircase railings, balconies, artistic fencing Elegant curves, integrated with fabrication, custom patterns and designs.
Art Installations Public art, gallery installations, architectural art features Large-scale bending, multiple materials, integration with other fabrication services.
Ornamental Elements Gates, screens, decorative panels, functional art pieces Precision bending combined with creative design, custom finishes and coatings.

Creative Collaboration

We work closely with artists, designers, and architects to bring creative visions to life. Our bending expertise can provide valuable feedback on feasibility, material selection, and technical approach to ensure your project is executed beautifully.

Our Approach

Phase What We Do
Concept Review Discuss your vision, sketch ideas, explore material and technique options
Technical Planning Work with your drawings or concepts to develop a production plan
Material Selection Recommend materials that work best for your design and desired finish
Production & Finishing Execute the bending with precision and handle finishing touches—grinding, painting, patina work

Materials & Finishes

We work with a wide variety of metals and can coordinate finishing treatments to achieve your desired aesthetic.

Materials

Carbon steel, stainless steel, aluminum, copper, brass, bronze, and specialized alloys

Finishing Options

Welding and fabrication integration, grinding and polishing, painting and powder coating, patina development, protective coatings
