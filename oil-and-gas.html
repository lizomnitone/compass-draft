Major Applications

We provide specialized metal bending solutions for critical oil and gas industry applications. Our precision work ensures reliability in the most demanding environments.

Application Typical Uses Bending Solutions
PLACEHOLDER: Pipelines Transportation lines, distribution networks, subsea risers, manifold connections Precision bends in carbon steel, stainless steel, and duplex materials. Custom radii and angles to specification. High-pressure rated bends with full traceability documentation. Bend radii from 1.5D to custom ratios. Quick turnaround available for field modifications.
PLACEHOLDER: Refining Process piping, heat exchanger tubes, fractionation units, process lines Specialized bends for refinery equipment. Materials include carbon steel and alloy steels. Bend radii optimized for compact installation. Corrosion-resistant coating options. Strict dimensional control and material certifications for all bends.
PLACEHOLDER: Drilling Drill string components, mud lines, kelly connections, flow lines Heavy-duty bends for drilling rig operations. Extreme pressure and temperature rated. Minimum bend radii for tight installations. High-cycle fatigue tested. Emergency expedited delivery available for unexpected field needs.
