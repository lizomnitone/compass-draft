Our Story

A History of Great Work

Colin Maskey started Compass Bending in 2004, coming from over 15 years of experience in the field. Our team prioritizes attention to detail, high quality standards, and a friendly attitude.

We strive for a personal service that meets the needs of our customers and goes beyond. We are open minded, and love challenging projects. We treat every customer, no matter the size of the job, as a long term opportunity for partnership.

With an experienced and professional team of over 30 employees, Compass Bending proudly serves customers all over North America.

Our Approach

Personal Service

We do not answer phones with a voicemail system – we provide a live, dedicated receptionist during business hours. If there is ever an issue with customers' jobs, we address it personally with two-way, live communication by phone or in person.

Research & Reliability

We do great amounts of research, which is how we've stayed steady and reliable through decades in business. This approach ensures we deliver consistent, dependable results every time.

Customer Benefit of Doubt

The customer always gets the benefit of doubt. We don't assume we're right. If something goes wrong, we identify the cause, remedy it, and work out something fair on a case-by-case basis.

Hire for Attitude & Skill

We hire for attitude and work ethic rather than formal credentials. Experience is optional. We believe in giving opportunities to the right candidates who demonstrate courtesy, positivity, and actual ability.

Long-Term Relationships

We think long-term with every customer. There is no small job – only the beginning of a relationship. We treat every person as though they can be a long-term customer or advocate.

Love Challenging Work

We love challenging jobs and don't complain about difficult work – these are the ones that get our adrenaline going. We love to put our minds to work and rise to the occasion.

Thoughtful Big Decisions

Big decisions take time and care. The bigger the decision, the more time we take. Our leadership gathers many opinions and considers the long-term implications before making major investments.
