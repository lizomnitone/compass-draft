Industrial Manufacturing

Precision bending solutions for manufacturing and equipment production.

Overview

Compass Bending partners with industrial manufacturers across multiple sectors, providing precision bending, fabrication, and assembly services. From equipment frames and hydraulic systems to complex structural components, we enable manufacturers to bring their designs to life reliably and on schedule.

Manufacturing Sectors We Serve

We partner with manufacturers and equipment makers across multiple industries, providing precision bending and fabrication support.

Heavy Equipment Manufacturing

Equipment frames, hydraulic piping, and structural components for construction machinery, excavators, loaders, and heavy industrial equipment.

Component Examples
Equipment Frames Chassis, structural support, attachment points
Hydraulic Systems Custom piping, support arms, manifold brackets
Structural Assemblies Boom assemblies, reinforcement, custom geometries

Fabrication & Metal Working Supply

We support metal fabrication shops and structural steel contractors by providing precision bending services on carbon steel, stainless steel, and aluminum.

Application Materials
Structural Steel Bending Carbon steel, stainless steel beams, channels, angles
Aluminum Components Frames, profiles, structural elements
Tube & Pipe Round, square, rectangular tube and pipe in various materials
