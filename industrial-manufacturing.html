Industrial Manufacturing

Precision bending solutions for manufacturing and equipment production.

Overview

Precision bending, fabrication, and assembly for industrial manufacturers. Equipment frames, hydraulic systems, structural components, and custom assemblies.

Industries We Serve

Category Description
Heavy Equipment Manufacturing Equipment frames, chassis, structural support systems, and attachment points for heavy machinery and construction equipment. Custom-engineered boom assemblies, reinforcement structures, and specialized geometries for oilfield and construction equipment. Structural assemblies built to withstand demanding field conditions and high-cycle operation.
Hydraulic Systems Custom piping, support arms, manifold brackets, and hose routing assemblies for hydraulic equipment and machinery. Complete manifold support systems and integration with equipment frames. Precision-bent hydraulic lines and connection components built to tight specifications and safety standards.
Fabrication & Metal Working Supply Structural steel bending for carbon steel and stainless steel beams, channels, and angles used in equipment and building applications. Aluminum components including frames, profiles, and structural elements for lightweight assemblies. Round, square, and rectangular tube and pipe in carbon steel, stainless, and specialty materials for exhaust systems, suspension components, and custom assemblies.
Advanced Systems Advanced machinery components requiring tight tolerances in aluminum, specialty alloys, and other precision-workable metals. Complex multi-plane bends, compound curves, and intricate geometries for specialized applications. Custom component design, prototyping, production runs, and in-house tooling development to support complete manufacturing solutions.
